=== HOW TO DECIDE ===

Use this priority order when comparing routes:

  3. MODERATE PHOTO CONCERNS: A few spots with potential issues — worth flagging, but not necessarily a dealbreaker.
  4. ENVIRONMENTAL RISKS: High collision history, active construction, and intense heat are important context.
  5. SHADE AND COMFORT: Prefer routes with more shade when other factors are similar, especially in hot weather.
